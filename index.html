About me

Welcome to my website! I am a Systems Design Engineering student at the University of Waterloo that's set to graduate in 2020. I love creating things, whether it be with my own two hands, with a couple strokes on the keyboard, or even with scissors and some glue. The first time I 3D printed a housing for my first year design project that I created on SolidWorks, I remember holding it in my hands and thinking "Wow! I made it and its not horrible!" Since then, I've learned more about the world of software and it feels like the only thing i've learned is how much more there is to learn! On this website you'll find a collection of past, present and future projects, I'll update it regularly as I make more things so stay tuned!

  • Software! After being introduced to coding in University (why didn't I take it in High School!) I found something so immensely satisfying about working through a problem, discovering the solution and having the computer do exactly what you expected. There is lots more for me to learn and I am constantly learning more and trying to make new things with the knowledge I have gained. In the words of one my friends from High School: "What's great about programming is that if I've done everything right, the script will do exactly what it was meant to do. The success of the program relies entirely on my ability to consider every problem that it may encounter. If the program doesn't work, it's entirely my fault. So when it does work, i know that I've done everything right"
